The Team

The people behind the pours. We love wine, we love our neighborhood, and we love sharing both with you.

Mandy

Owner

Mandy loves wine with a special affinity for Burgundy. Her knowledge comes from years of being a small business owner in the industry and of course drinking copious amounts of the stuff. She spends most of her days running the business, chatting with customers, cleaning glasses and anything else that needs to get done.

Tara

Wine Club Lead

Tara is both the heart and the driving force behind our wine club. With an incredible depth of knowledge and a remarkable palate, she curates thoughtful, standout selections every month. A major part of the Bouchon team, she elevates everything we do truly the best of the best.

Kevin

Bottle Shop Guru

Kevin is the perfect Saturday guy at Bouchon - super chill and knows a lot. He's worked in the wine industry in all its capacities. He started in wine distribution where he transitioned to buyer and manager at Rocket Market, Spokane's premier wine shop. He recently moved to Portland and immediately found a home at Bouchon.

Brennen

Hospitality Hero

Brennen is our newest and friendliest staff member yet! She truly is ready to help whenever she can. Her wine journey has just begun but she is learning fast and studying hard. If you need someone to lift your spirits we recommend Brennen :)
